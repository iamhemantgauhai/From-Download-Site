frequently asked questions

You Have Questions, We Have Answers

Glass related problems can be extremely frustrating, especially when it means you cant use your windows, doors, tables or all of them. Have any Questions?

How much does it cost to replace a window glass that has been broken?

Prices of glass replacement may vary depending on the situations such as type of glass, job complications and timings.

What does it cost to replace a window pane?

It depends if just the glass can be re-glazed by simply replacing the pane. Typically our glazier assesses the damage and glass size. Prices may be higher for emergency repair.

Is it less expensive to replace the glass or the entire window?

Replacing glass will always be cheaper as it's a simple fix. However, the window replacement is a long-term fix. Cost factor will always be higher than replacing just the glass.

How long will it take you to repair or replace the broken window or door pane?

The size of the door or window determines the answer. A door takes about 60 minutes to install on average, and an accessible aluminium window takes about 45 minutes. We'll need someone to be there during the project because we usually glaze from the inside.

What happens if the replacement glass isn't available right away?

When you use the customized glass there are huge changes that we would not be able to replace the glass right away. Our first priority at that time is to secure the damage area ( shop front, window or door) with a strong cardboard or safety board till the time we prepare the custom made glass for you.
